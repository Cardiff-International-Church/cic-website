Our Commitment to Community

Cardiff International Church is committed to serving our local and international community through free educational and cultural programs. Our classes provide opportunities to learn new skills, practice languages, and build meaningful connections in a welcoming, multicultural environment.

All our classes are completely free of charge and open to everyone, regardless of background, faith, or nationality. We believe in creating bridges between cultures and helping people thrive in Cardiff.

How to Get Started

Just Come Along

No registration required! Simply show up at the times listed. All our classes welcome drop-ins and new participants at any time.

Meet the Teachers

Our friendly volunteer teachers and class coordinators will help you get settled and find the right level or activity for you.

Learn & Connect

Participate at your own pace, make new friends, and enjoy the multicultural learning environment we've created.

Become Part of Community

Many of our class participants become regular members of our church family. You're always welcome to join our Sunday services and other activities.

Ready to Join Us?

Take the first step towards learning something new and making meaningful connections. Our doors are always open, and we can't wait to meet you!
