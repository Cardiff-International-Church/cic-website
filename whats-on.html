Join Us Throughout the Week

Experience worship, fellowship, and community with believers from around the world

Cardiff International Church offers several regular gatherings throughout the week where you can worship, grow in faith, and build community with believers from around the world.

Each gathering has its own unique character and purpose, but all are centered on God's love and our international fellowship.
