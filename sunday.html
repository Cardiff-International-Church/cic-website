Unity in Diversity

Our Sunday service may vary from week to week, as we embrace the diversity of our transient international congregation.

With members from different church traditions and cultural backgrounds, the style of worship, expressions of praise, and flow of service can look different, reflecting the rich variety of God's family.

It has been a true blessing for us to experience unity in diversity, coming together as one big family in the love of God.
