Special Events

Special Events

Trips & Weekend Away

CIC organises day trips for sightseeing, hiking, and picnics, allowing us to explore the beautiful Welsh culture and heritage.

We also plan occasional weekend getaways for fellowship, providing an opportunity to connect and grow together in a relaxed and enjoyable setting.

International Night

We hold international events from time to time, where you can enjoy a variety of national dishes, songs, and music, as well as presentations and stories from different parts of the world.

It's a fun and educational experience, allowing us to connect with and appreciate diverse cultures and histories.
