Our Pastor

Pastor

Meet our pastoral leadership serving Cardiff International Church

Rev. Gi-Jung Song

Ps GJ Song graduated with a Bachelor of Theology (BTh) from Hansei University and a Master of Theology (MTh) from Wales Evangelical School of Theology (2001-2002, now Union School of Theology). He resigned from his military career as a captain in 1992 and was born again and baptised at Yoido Full Gospel Church. His ministry journey includes serving at Grace & Truth Church, Highfields Church, Brackla Tabernacle, as well as missions across Europe in countries like the UK (Wales), Latvia, Ukraine, etc.

He was one of seven delegates representing Wales at the 4th Lausanne Congress, which took place in South Korea in 2024. He organised the Wales & Korea Celebration 2016, commemorating the 150th anniversary of R. J. Thomas' mission. Ps GJ is the founder and pastor of Cardiff International Church (CIC), serving since 2008, and is also the pastor of Newport International Church (NIC) from 2021 to the present.

Personal Life

Ps GJ Song is blessed to be married to Ji-Hee Lee, who also serves on the CIC Leadership Team.
