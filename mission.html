Mission

Fulfilling the Great Commission

Sharing God's love locally in Wales and internationally around the world

Cardiff International Church is committed to fulfilling the Great Commission.

As part of our mission, we coordinate mission trips and build partnerships with evangelical church networks, mission organisations, short-term mission teams and training groups/Bible schools.

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age."

Matthew 28:18-20
