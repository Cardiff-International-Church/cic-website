Beyond the Leadership Team, many members serve throughout the year in different ministries. Some also join the team for training and discipleship, growing in faith and developing leadership skills.

At our church, members serve and build up the body of Christ according to God's calling and the spiritual gifts He has given each of us (1 Cor 12; Rom 12; Eph 4).

At CIC, we believe every person is a vital part of the body of Christ. As we serve one another, we grow stronger together in faith.
