Foundational Era

Photo News 2008-2010

The early years of establishing our international community

The foundational years of Cardiff International Church, when our vision of being "a channel of God's love & blessing to all nations" was taking shape. These years marked the beginning of our multicultural fellowship and the establishment of our core values and mission.
