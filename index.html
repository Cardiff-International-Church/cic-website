Welcome to CIC

Welcome

A warm welcome from all of us at Cardiff International Church (CIC)!

Whether you're studying, working, or living in Cardiff, the capital of Wales, we are here to be your home and family.

We meet on Sundays and weekdays for a variety of activities, and you are most welcome to join us! We are conveniently located near Cardiff University.

Looking forward to seeing you soon!
